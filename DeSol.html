Photographic Context

These photographs by NY Photographer, Peter Karp are some of the most important to be taken in the 21st century.

In May of 2011, a temporary sculpture garden of work by Sol Lewitt was constructed for public consumption. In December of that year the show was over and the concrete blocked pyramid (Tower (Columbus))was removed. The photos are as revealing as the original work.
